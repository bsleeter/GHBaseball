Day 6 — Practice

Monday, March 9, 2026
Sehmel Park — Field #1
Varsity: 2:30 – 5:00 PM

Practice Flow

2:30 PM
Stretch & Throw
Dynamic stretching followed by partner throwing progression. Build arm strength and get loose.
2:55 PM
Daily Position Work
Break into positional groups for daily skill work and fundamental reps.

Infielders

  • Ground ball work
  • Feeds & turns
  • Footwork reps

Outfielders

  • Fly ball reads
  • Routes & catches
  • Throwing to bases

Catchers

  • Blocking
  • Receiving
  • Transfer & throws
3:20 PM
Team Bunting Practice
Full team bunting work. Every hitter gets reps while defense works bunt coverage assignments.

Offense

  • Sacrifice bunt technique
  • Push bunts & drag bunts
  • Squeeze bunt reps

Defense

  • Bunt coverage rotations
  • Communication & reads
  • Fielding & throwing accuracy
3:50 PM
Live At-Bats
Hitters go live against our pitchers. Game-speed at-bats with full counts. Pitchers work on commanding the zone, hitters compete with an approach.

Pitchers

  • Full arsenal, game intensity
  • Work ahead in counts
  • Compete to get outs

Hitters

  • Quality at-bats
  • Swing decisions
  • Situational approach
4:45 PM
Conditioning & Wrap Up
Team conditioning, gather equipment, and debrief.
5:00 PM
End
Dismiss.
