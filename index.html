About Tides Baseball

The Gig Harbor High School Tides Baseball program is committed to developing student-athletes both on and off the field. Our program competes in the South Puget Sound League (3A) and fields Varsity, JV, and C Team squads each spring.

We emphasize fundamentals, teamwork, sportsmanship, and academic achievement. Our mission is to provide every player with a positive, competitive experience that builds character and a lifelong love of the game.

Home games are played at Sehmel Homestead Park. The 2026 season begins with tryouts on March 2nd.
